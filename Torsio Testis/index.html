    Torsio Testis

    Definisi

    Torsio testis adalah kondisi medis darurat yang terjadi ketika testis (buah zakar) berputar pada hubungannya, yaitu spermatic cord dalam tunika vaginalis. Kondisi ini memerlukan perhatian medis segera.1,2

    Gambar 1. (A) Torsio testis terjadi ketika tali pusat terpuntir dan menghentikan aliran darah1 (B) Anatomi torsio testis terjadi ketika testis berputar di dalam tunica vaginalis. Pasien dengan deformitas bell clapper mengalami kondisi ini2

    Anamnesis

    As with testicular torsion, patients with torsion of an appendage complain of scrotal pain but report milder symptoms, with a more gradual onset. they report nausea, vomiting, urinary symptoms, or previous episodes of similar pain less commonly than patients with testicular torsion. ey usually seek medical attention later than patients with testicular torsion, generally aer 48 hours of symptoms.2 On physical examination, twisting of the appendix testis leads to formation of a hard, tender, 2-to 3- mm nodule at the upper pole of the testicle. Unlike in testicular torsion, the entire testicle is not tender. e testicle also does not change in overall size, and the scrotum typically does not swell until late in the disease process. e cremasteric reex typically is intact. On transillumination, the ischemic appendage may rarely be seen as a blue dot.2

    Pemeriksaan Penunjang

    Urinalysis should not show evidence of infection. On ultrasound imaging, the appendix under torsion will appear hypoechoic. Color Doppler ultrasound can show decreased ow in normal and torsed appendages. With torsion of the appendix, a hypoechoic spherical nodule with a diameter more than 5 mm is present over the superior aspect of the testicle. Gambar

    Differential diagnosis of the acute scrotum.

    Torsion of the testicular appendage
    Acute epididymitis/epididymo-orchitis
    Scrotal trauma or hematoma
    Incarcerated inguinal hernia
    Ureteral calculi

    (Henoch-Schönlein purpura)

    Testicular tumors
    Scrotal cellulitis
    Scrotal fat necrosis Idiopathic4

    Tatalaksana

    If testicular torsion is ruled out, surgical excision of the appendix is rarely necessary. Treatment consists of scrotal support, ice packs, and NSAIDs. Resolution of symptoms can be expected within 7 to 10 days. Surgical excision is reserved for uncontrollable pain.2

    Gambar. (A) Torsi testis lebih sering terjadi ke arah medial. Cobalah untuk melakukan detorsi dengan memutar testis ke arah luar menuju paha. Ini paling berhasil jika dicoba dalam beberapa jam pertama torsi, sebelum timbul pembengkakan skrotum yang signifikan. Narkotika intravena (misalnya fentanyl) dapat diberikan atau blok kabel dilakukan sebelum mencoba detorsi.2 (B) Manuver detorsi [Testis kanan]. Detorsi testis mungkin memerlukan rotasi testis melalui dua bidang. Untuk melepaskan otot cremasteric, putar testis dalam arah caudal-ke-cranial secara bersamaan dengan rotasi medial-ke-lateral.2 (C) Detorsi manual testis dilakukan dengan mengingat arah untuk detorsi, seperti membuka buku. Titik akhir dari manuver ini adalah penghilangan rasa sakit. Jika rasa sakit semakin parah, coba lakukan detorsi ke arah yang berlawanan. Jika detorsi manual berhasil (yaitu, rasa sakit berkurang), konsultasi darurat dengan urologis masih diperlukan.3
